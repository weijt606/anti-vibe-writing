# Example 3: Twitter / X post

Scenario preset: tweet. Mode: default.

## Before

```
🚀 Excited to share a new tool we've been working on!

In today's fast-paced world of AI-generated content, it can be challenging
to maintain authenticity and a human touch in your writing. That's why we
built Anti-Vibe Writing — a powerful, intuitive solution designed to help
you transform generic AI drafts into polished, engaging content that
resonates with your audience.

✨ Key benefits:
• Removes AI-generated patterns
• Enhances readability
• Maintains your unique voice

Try it out today! 👇
```

## After

```
AI drafts that read like AI drafts have a problem. Not that they're wrong.
They're just clearly assembled. Anti-Vibe Writing is the final pass that
removes the assembly marks.

Works in English and Chinese.
```

## What changed

- Emoji preamble cut
- "In today's fast-paced world" gone
- Three benefit bullets condensed into one sharp claim
- No "try it out today"; the post earns the click on its own
- Length dropped from 110+ words to 30, which fits Twitter's actual rhythm
- Final short line ("Works in English and Chinese") adds a concrete fact instead of a CTA
